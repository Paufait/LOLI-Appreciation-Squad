Palewave

A style which becomes more popular during the summer, the Palewave aesthetic revolves around light, pale colours in order to show off a relaxed or comfortable vibe. Desaturated, minimalistic pieces such as lightwash jeans, light pastelle-coloured tees and white shoes such as Adidas Stan Smiths are staples in a Palewave wardrobe. With its minimal need to show labeling or branding, the Palewave aesthetic is quite inexpensive to portray.
