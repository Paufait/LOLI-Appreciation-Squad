Supreme

Founded in 1994, Supreme established a skateboard and clothing shop in New York City. The brand caters to a youth culture involved with skateboarding, hip hop and punk rock. Their style of releasing clothing is on a weekly basis, where every Thursday at 11AM (EST), they release a small set on their website until the end of the season, although pieces are often sold out within the hour. Supreme sells a wide variety of clothing from to toe. Some of their most valuable pieces (non-collaborations) can go up to $1000 on secondary markets. Throughout the years, Supreme has collaborated with many other brands, such as Nike, Air Jordan, The North Face, Playboy, Comme des Garcons, Stone Island, and most notably, Louis Vuitton.

Their collaboration with Louis Vuitton is the brand’s first opportunity into creating their styles with high end fashion designers and materials. Despite the release coming in July, prices have been already leaked. Supreme has showcased pieces that averaged thousands of dollars retail. It’s most expensive piece is the trunk, priced at a hefty $68,500 USD.

Rick Owens

Richard Saturnino Owens created his brand under his own name but shortened; Rick Owens. Rick is known for his dark, gothic, and grunge style of clothing. Throughout the late 1990’s and the early 2000’s Rick’s designs were often sold to other companies until his talent was recognized, where he was given multiple awards for fashion design. Eventually after being sponsored by Vogue, he began showing off his designs on catwalks in France. After having many of his pieces were own by celebrities such as Kate Moss, he began opening boutiques in places such as Paris, London, New York City, Tokyo, and Hong Kong.

In the streetwear world, Rick Owens is known for his collection called ‘DRKSHDW’, featuring his signature dark, fitting pieces, but mixed with androgynous features such as skirts and leather. His footwear has also left an imprint on streetwear as a whole, with pieces such as the infamous Geobaskets, and the classic black hightop.
