Techwear

A controversial aesthetic, Techwear emphasizes a futuristic look combined with technical benefits to everyday life. Created with special fabrics and unique construction, Techwear pieces allow for more breathability, water-resistance and comfort. The difficulty with Techwear is its unique look. Often not modeled for a typical 9-5 job or casual dinner, but rather if you were a ninja that traveled from the future. The special fabric and construction also guarantees a fairly expensive price tag. Creating a wardrobe with some of the most well known brands such as Stone Island, Goretex, ACRONYM, and Y-3, you can find yourself spending over $1000 for a pair of pants, let alone a single fit.
