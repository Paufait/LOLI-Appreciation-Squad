Hypebeast

The first aesthetic to come to mind when discussing streetwear, Hypebeast is a style which revolves around the brands of clothing being worn. Rather than emphasizing on a particular style or colour scheme, the aesthetic emphasizes on the hype and price of the pieces. Brands such as Supreme, Off-White, Vlone, Yeezy, and Bape are worn by popular celebrities, causing an increase in demand which in turn increases pricing within the second-hand market.
